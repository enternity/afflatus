# BIT MANIPULATION

## Bitwise Operators
| Operator  |  Syntax | Example| 
|:---|:---:|:---|
|  **OR** | `|`  |   `1101 | 0110 = 1111` (13 | 6 = 15)
|  **AND** | `&`  |  `1101 & 0110 = 0100` (13 & 6 = 4)|  
| **XOR**  | `^`  |  `1101 ^ 0110 = 1011` (13 ^ 6 = 11)|
|**NOT**| `~`| `~1101 = 0010` (~13 = 2)|
